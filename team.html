Uzor is a meta-leader with over nineteen (19) years of experience solving complex problems in the public and private sectors in industries such as Health, Insurance, Technology, Logistics etc. Uzor is also the CEO of Famacare Centre – a network of efficient, digitally driven clinics delivering Africa’s solution to the gapping Primary healthcare delivery challenge. Whilst at Interswitch, she designed the framework for Nigeria’s first fully integrated health information management solution – Smarthealth.

Uzor brings her acumen and expertise in business development, establishing new partnerships and guiding clients with making best decisions for growth. She currently has innovative projects with numerous government agencies creating sustainable healthcare solutions with appropriate financing models especially in Primary and Specialised Healthcare. Uzor is currently pursuing a Doctorate in Business Administration at Hult International Business School and has a Master’s in Health Economics and Management from the University of Sheffield, UK.

She has a certificate in Leading in Healthcare Systems from Harvard University, USA and a Bachelor’s degree in Microbiology from the University of Lagos, Nigeria. She is a CPHIMS certified Product Manager and a lean six sigma green belt. Uzor hones vision and has the capacity to link and leverage people and resources. She enjoys travel, music and capacity building.
